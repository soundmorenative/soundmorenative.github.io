Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

This privacy policy explains how SoundMoreNative ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our website (soundmorenative.github.io) and our use of third-party APIs to publish content to our own social media accounts.

1. Who we are

SoundMoreNative is a small content brand based in the United Kingdom that produces short-form video lessons teaching authentic British English. We publish content on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.

2. Information we collect

This website does not collect personal information from visitors. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts on this site.

If you contact us by email, we will hold your email address and the contents of your message solely to respond to your enquiry.

3. Use of third-party APIs

SoundMoreNative uses the official developer APIs provided by TikTok, Google (YouTube), and Meta (Instagram) for the sole purpose of publishing video content to our own accounts on those platforms. Specifically:

  • We authenticate to each platform using OAuth credentials issued to our own SoundMoreNative accounts.
  • We use these APIs only to upload, schedule, and manage content on accounts we own and operate.
  • We do not access, collect, store, or process data belonging to any other user of those platforms.
  • We do not use these APIs to read, scrape, or analyse content posted by other creators.

Access tokens issued by these platforms are stored locally on the operator's own machine and used only to make authorised API calls. They are not shared with any third party.

4. Information you may share with the platforms

When you watch, like, comment on, or follow our content on TikTok, YouTube, or Instagram, the data you generate is collected and processed by those platforms in accordance with their own privacy policies, not ours. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of:

  • TikTok
  • Google / YouTube
  • Instagram / Meta

5. Data we do not handle

SoundMoreNative does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data of viewers, followers, or commenters. We do not run a mailing list. We do not operate any backend service that holds user data.

6. Children's privacy

Our content is intended for adult and teenage learners of English. We do not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13.

7. Your rights

If you have contacted us by email and would like us to delete the email correspondence we hold from you, please contact us using the address below. Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of personal data we hold about you.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at reecedalgassocial@gmail.com.
