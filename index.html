SoundMoreNative

British English for people tired of sounding textbook 🇬🇧

SoundMoreNative produces short-form video content teaching authentic British English to intermediate and advanced learners. We focus on the gap between textbook English and how British people actually speak — greetings, idioms, pronunciation quirks, and the cultural context that helps learners sound native rather than just fluent.

What we cover

  • Real British greetings and how to respond to them
  • Idioms and expressions native speakers actually use
  • The unspoken rules of British conversation
  • Pronunciation patterns and regional notes
